    Shortcuts:
    LF- Large Furniture ATK - Attack CR - Crit Rating MR - Magic Resist IS - Insight AS - Attack Speed
    SF - Small Furniture HP - Health ACC - Accuracy LL - Life Leech TY - Tenacity PP- Physical Pierce
    HF - Hanging Furniture DEF - Defense HR - Health Recovery CDA - Crit Damage Aplification RH - Recived Healing MP - Magic Pierce
    CDR - Crit Damage Resistance CBR - Crit Block Rate
    Note:
    Fields marked in green indicate the additiona l value that the furniture will give after buffs. However, it is worth noting that the additional buffs are slightly weaker than this original value
    For example, Athelia in its basic stats from one +3 furniture can draw 3.2% ATK, the same bonus to ATK after additional buffs is only 2.4%.
